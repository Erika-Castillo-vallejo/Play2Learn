Game 1

Anagram Hunts

“Anagram Hunts” a word game that challenges players to rearrange the letters of a given word to form as many valid words as possible. For example, from the word “hunts,” players can derive words like “shunt,” “hunt,” “nuts,” and “thus.” The goal is to identify the highest number of combinations within a limited time, testing your linguistic skills and mental agility.

Math Facts Practice

“Math Facts Practice” an educational game designed to help students enhance their fluency in basic mathematical operations, including addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Through interactive activities and progressively challenging levels, players can strengthen their math skills in an engaging and effective manner. The game aims to provide a tool that facilitates consistent practice of fundamental math concepts, adapting to each user’s individual learning pace.
